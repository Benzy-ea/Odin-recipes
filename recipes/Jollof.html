Jollof rice

Description

This is the recipe of a beautiful jollof rice. The amazing rice that has sparked debate amongst many Africans

But however this is the recipe of the Nigerian Jollof rice. Arguably the best amongst them all.

Ingredients

  • Rice ofcourse
  • Tomato
  • Boiled meat water
  • maggi
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Onions
  • garlic
  • curry
  • Boiled Meat
  • Pepper
  • Green pepper
  • peas
  • carrot

Steps

  1. Per-boil the rice by boiling the rice for a few minutes -- not fully boiled-- before upending it in a seive to drain.
  2. Wash the rice by placing the perboiled rice in a bowl of water and slowly-- using your hands or preferably a smaller seive-- scooping the rice into another bowl without water until you can see the bottom. This is to get rid of stone particles that may have settled at the bottom.
  3. Pour oil into the pot in proportion to the rice perboiled
  4. Pour blended pepper into the oil. If you dont already have blended pepper, no need to worry. Pick a few and blend it.
  5. Pour sliced onions into the hot oil
  6. Pour Tomato in proportion to the rice into the oil
  7. Stir for a few minutes
  8. Pour boiled meat water into the fried Tomato in proportion to the Tomato and rice
  9. Pour rice into the Tomato and Meat water
  10. Stir slightly
  11. Close pot for a 5 minutes

    12. make sure water covers the surface of the rice just slightly

  12. After the 5 minutes is up pour sliced green pepper
  13. Leave to cook
  14. Check after 15-25 minutes
  15. If rice tastes properly cooked --soft-- turn the rice with a wooden spoon from the bottom of the pot.
  16. Allow to steam for 3 minutes after adding carrots and green peas
  17. Your jollof is ready.
